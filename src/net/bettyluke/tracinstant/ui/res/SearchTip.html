Tips:

Separate search terms using a space.
Right-click a table column heading to quickly add terms.

Examples:

peter -paul Tickets containing Peter but not Paul (anywhere)
cc:luke Search column 'cc' for luke
pri:low Search column 'priority'; matches 'low' and 'lowest'
owner: Tickets without an owner
fred -cc:fred Tickets containing Fred, but where Fred is not CC'd

Regular expressions:

. Match any character
c* , c+Match character c zero or more times , one or more times
^ , $Start of line , End of line
\b , \BWord boundary , Not a word boundary
\w\s+\SA word character, then whitespace(s), then a non-whitespace character

Bigger Example:

st:-cl ^high key:\bwiki\b key:help|docum
Active, high(est) priority, containing keywords ("wiki" AND (help OR documentation))
